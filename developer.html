Descripción del producto

Meridian is a preventive health analytics platform that integrates clinical biomarkers, body composition, and physiological data to help users understand their biological health trajectory. The platform analyzes laboratory markers, fitness capacity, and wearable-derived physiological signals — including sleep quality, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels — to generate longitudinal health insights and personalized clinical recommendations.

The Meridian Age™ index is a composite score that combines three evidence-based dimensions: biological age derived from blood biomarkers (PhenoAge, Levine et al. 2018), cardiovascular age from aerobic capacity (VO₂ Max), and body composition age from DEXA measurements. Wearable data enriches this index with continuous physiological signals between annual clinical assessments.

Uso de datos wearables

Declaración de uso

Wearable data is used exclusively to contextualize biomarker findings and provide longitudinal health insights. Metrics such as sleep duration, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels are analyzed alongside laboratory biomarkers to monitor physiological trends over time. Data is displayed directly to the user within their personal health report and is not shared with third parties, used for advertising, or applied to train machine learning models.

Users explicitly authorize wearable data access via OAuth 2.0 authentication and can revoke access at any time from their Meridian account or directly from the wearable provider's application.

Datos solicitados

Meridian solicita únicamente los datos estrictamente necesarios para la funcionalidad del servicio. No se solicitan datos de ubicación, contactos ni ningún dato no relacionado con salud.

Usuarios objetivo

  • Adultos entre 30 y 65 años interesados en salud preventiva y longevidad
  • Profesionales con alto nivel de actividad física y gestión del estrés
  • Pacientes en programas de salud corporativa y bienestar ejecutivo
  • Deportistas y entusiastas del fitness con interés en biomarcadores clínicos

Escala esperada

  • Fase piloto (2026): 50–100 usuarios
  • Año 1 (2026–2027): ~500–1.000 usuarios en Santiago, Chile
  • Año 2–3: expansión a Colombia y México, potencial de varios miles de usuarios

Almacenamiento y seguridad de datos

User data is stored securely in encrypted databases hosted on servers with industry-standard security practices. All wearable data is accessed through user-authorized OAuth connections. Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored encrypted at rest and are never exposed in client-side code or public repositories. All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS.

Users can revoke data access at any time. Upon revocation, Meridian calls the provider's deauthorization endpoint and purges stored tokens within 24 hours. Full account deletion is completed within 30 days of request.
