Product Description

Meridian is a physician-led preventive health analytics platform that integrates clinical biomarkers, body composition, and physiological data to help users understand their biological health trajectory. The platform analyzes laboratory markers, aerobic fitness capacity, and wearable-derived physiological signals, including sleep quality, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels, to generate longitudinal health insights and personalized clinical recommendations.

The Meridian Age™ index is a composite biological age score combining three evidence-based dimensions: biological age derived from blood biomarkers (PhenoAge, Levine et al. 2018), cardiovascular age from aerobic capacity (VO₂ Max), and body composition age from DEXA measurements. Wearable data enriches this index with continuous physiological signals between annual clinical assessments, enabling users to track the real-time impact of lifestyle interventions.

The platform operates under the medical supervision of Dr. Alejandro Berlin, MD, MSc, Full Professor at the University of Toronto and staff physician at University Health Network (UHN), with operations in Santiago, Chile.

How Wearable Data Is Used

Wearable data is used exclusively to contextualize biomarker findings and provide longitudinal health insights to the individual user. Metrics such as sleep duration, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels are analyzed alongside laboratory biomarkers to monitor physiological trends over time.

Data is displayed directly to the authorized user within their personal health report and is never shared with other users, third parties, or advertisers. Wearable data is not used to determine insurance eligibility, credit scoring, or employment.

Users explicitly authorize wearable data access via OAuth 2.0 authentication on a per-platform basis and can revoke access at any time from their Meridian account or directly from the wearable provider's application.

Data Requested

Meridian requests only the data strictly necessary for the functionality visible to the user. No location data, contacts, financial data, or any health data unrelated to physiological monitoring is requested. Specific scopes vary by platform.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored securely in encrypted databases hosted on servers with industry-standard security practices. All wearable data is accessed exclusively through user-authorized OAuth 2.0 connections. Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored encrypted at rest on the server and are never exposed in client-side code or public repositories. All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS.

Users can revoke data access at any time. Upon revocation, Meridian calls the provider's deauthorization endpoint and purges stored tokens within 24 hours. Full account deletion removes all personal data within 30 days of request.
