1. Descripción del producto (PEGAR EN TODOS)

Meridian is a preventive health analytics platform that integrates clinical biomarkers, body composition, and physiological data to help users understand their biological health trajectory. The platform analyzes laboratory markers, fitness capacity, and wearable-derived physiological signals, such as sleep quality, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels, to generate longitudinal health insights and personalized clinical recommendations. The platform is operated by Dr. Alejandro Berlin, MD, MSc, Full Professor at the University of Toronto and staff physician at University Health Network (UHN).

2. Descripción del uso de datos wearables (PEGAR EN TODOS EXCEPTO STRAVA)

Wearable data will be used to contextualize biomarker findings and provide longitudinal health insights to the individual user. Metrics such as sleep duration, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and activity levels will be analyzed alongside laboratory biomarkers to monitor physiological trends over time. Data is displayed directly to the authorized user within their personal health report and is not shared with third parties. Wearable data is not used for advertising purposes. With explicit, separate user opt-in consent, anonymized physiological data may be used to improve Meridian's health models. Users can revoke access at any time.

2b. Uso de datos: STRAVA ESPECÍFICO (SOLO PARA EL FORMULARIO DE STRAVA)

Strava activity data is displayed exclusively to the authenticated user within their personal health report to complement clinical biomarker analysis. Activity data (type, duration, heart rate) is shown only to the authorized user. Strava data is never used in any AI, machine learning, or analytical model. No data is displayed to other users. No commercial redistribution of Strava data. Full compliance with Strava API Terms of Service including the prohibition on AI/ML use.

3. Datos solicitados (ADAPTAR POR PLATAFORMA)

Sleep metrics (duration, stages, efficiency) · Heart rate (resting and continuous) · Heart rate variability (HRV / RMSSD) · Physical activity and workouts · Steps and active calories · Estimated VO₂ Max (where available) · Basal body temperature (where available) · User profile (age, biological sex) for demographic context

4. Usuarios objetivo (PEGAR EN TODOS)

Health-conscious adults aged 30–65 interested in preventive medicine and longevity · Professionals in corporate wellness programs · Athletes and fitness enthusiasts interested in clinical biomarkers · Patients enrolled in personalized health monitoring programs under physician supervision

5. Escala esperada (PEGAR EN TODOS)

Pilot phase (2026): 50–100 users · Year 1 (2026–2027): 500–1,000 users in Santiago, Chile · Long-term: several thousand users across Chile, Colombia, and Mexico

6. Almacenamiento de datos (PEGAR EN TODOS)

User data is stored securely in encrypted databases. All wearable data is accessed through user-authorized OAuth connections. Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest on the server and are never exposed in client-side code or public repositories. All communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Users can revoke data access at any time. Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days.

Textos específicos por plataforma

Garmin: Campo "Describe your use case"

Meridian integrates Garmin Health API data as the fitness pillar of the Meridian Age index, a composite biological age score combining blood biomarkers, body composition, and physiological fitness. Garmin-derived VO₂ Max, HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep data provide continuous physiological context between annual clinical assessments. Use case: remote patient monitoring and population health. Target market: preventive medicine and corporate wellness in Chile and Latin America. Pilot: 50 patients under physician supervision.

Fitbit: Campo "App description"

Meridian is a physician-led preventive health platform. Fitbit data (resting heart rate, HRV, sleep stages, activity) is used alongside clinical laboratory biomarkers to generate a longitudinal biological age score (Meridian Age™). Data is displayed exclusively to the authorized user within their personal health report. No advertising use. No data sale. No AI/ML training. Use case: preventive medicine and wellness. Physician responsible: Dr. Alejandro Berlin, MD, MSc.

Oura: Campo "What will you build?"

Meridian integrates Oura readiness, sleep, and HRV data to enrich a physician-supervised biological age index. Oura provides the highest-quality sleep and recovery data for the Meridian platform's recovery pillar. Data is used exclusively for individual user health reporting under physician supervision. Pilot: 50 users in Santiago, Chile.

Meridian uses WHOOP recovery score, HRV, sleep, and strain data alongside clinical laboratory biomarkers to compute a longitudinal biological age index (Meridian Age™). This allows users to track the impact of lifestyle interventions on their biological health trajectory between annual clinical assessments. Physician responsible: Dr. Alejandro Berlin, MD, MSc, University of Toronto.

Nota sobre Open Wearables (openwearables.io)

Open Wearables (MIT licensed, self-hosted) actualmente soporta: Garmin, Fitbit, Oura, WHOOP, Strava, Apple Health. Polar y Suunto NO estan incluidos aun en Open Wearables; requieren integracion directa via Polar AccessLink API y Suunto Cloud API respectivamente. Considerar Open Wearables como capa de unificacion para las primeras 6 plataformas; integrar Polar y Suunto directamente o via Thryve/ROOK.
